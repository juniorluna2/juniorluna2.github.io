Rules

  • Event will be played under USGA rules
  • Max Score: Net Triple Bogey (ie. Par 5 with stroke would be a gross score of 9 / net 8)
  • For "Match Play" formats, all players must finish out each hole until "max score" is reached
  • All putts within one (1) foot or tweleve (12) inches can be concede by opponents

    • Payment

    Ideally, if you can bring cash on Saturday, that would be best. Money will be collect on Saturday morning. If you are unable to, please send payment in some sort of electronic form (PayPal, Zelle, Venmo) before Saturday, September 10th, 2022.
    Winner will be paid out after final round!!!

    Ties

    There will be absolutely no ties!! After the conclusion of the final round, a playoff will procede starting on a hole 1 and will continue until a winner is crowned.
