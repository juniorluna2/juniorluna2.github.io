Rules:
Each Participant will select four (4) players from the list provided below. A random draft order will be conduct and order of draft will be a snake draft (ie. Round 1: 1 2 3 4, Round 2: 4 3 2 1 and so on...). The particiant with the lowest accumulative score will be the winner. Important: Each player that misses the cut will add fifteen (15) strokes to the participant overall's score. An example would be if the four (4) players selected, my accumulative score is -10 but two (2) players miss the cut, then my adjusted accumulative score would be +20.
